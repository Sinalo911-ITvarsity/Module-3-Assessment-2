Lime Cheesecake

Prep time: 3 hrs 55 mins

Yield: 8-10

Price: R50,00

Description

The "Lime Cheesecake" is a tangy and decadent dessert that combines the creamy richness of cheesecake with the refreshing zing of lime. This delightful treat features a buttery graham cracker crust that provides the perfect base for the creamy lime-infused filling. The cheesecake is baked to perfection until it's just set, resulting in a smooth and velvety texture. Finally, it's topped with a tangy lime curd or zest for an extra burst of citrus flavor. Whether enjoyed on its own or paired with fresh berries, this lime cheesecake is sure to be a crowd-pleaser at any gathering.
