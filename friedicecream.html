Fried Ice-Cream

Prep time: 25 mins

Yield: 8

Price: R65,00

Description

"Fried ice cream" is a unique dessert that combines the crispy texture of fried batter with the creamy goodness of ice cream. Typically, a scoop of ice cream is quickly frozen and then coated in a mixture of crushed cornflakes, cinnamon, and sugar. This coated ice cream ball is then deep-fried for a brief moment, creating a crispy shell while keeping the ice cream inside frozen. The result is a delightful contrast of hot and cold, crispy and creamy, making fried ice cream a popular and indulgent treat in many cuisines around the world.
