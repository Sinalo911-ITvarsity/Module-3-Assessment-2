Nanami Gyoza

Prep time: 1 hr

Yield: 6

Price: R60,00

Description

Nanami gyoza is a Japanese dumpling dish that combines the savory flavors of minced meat (often pork or chicken) with vegetables such as cabbage, garlic, and ginger, all wrapped in a thin dough wrapper. These dumplings are typically pan-fried until golden brown and crispy on one side, then steamed to ensure the filling is cooked through. Nanami gyoza are often served with a dipping sauce made from soy sauce, rice vinegar, and chili oil, adding a tangy and spicy kick to each bite. These dumplings are popular appetizers or snacks in Japan and are enjoyed for their satisfying texture and flavorful filling.
