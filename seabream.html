Sea bream ceviche with charred corn

Prep time: 30 mins

Yield: 2

Price: R80,00

Description

Sea bream ceviche with charred corn is a light and flavorful dish featuring thinly sliced sea bream marinated in citrus juices, paired with charred corn for added sweetness and texture. This refreshing appetizer combines zesty acidity with smoky undertones, creating a perfect balance of flavors.
