Steak Tartare

Prep time: 15 mins

Yield: 1

Price: R50,00

Description

Steak tartare is a traditional European dish consisting of finely chopped raw beef seasoned with spices, herbs, and condiments. It's often served as an appetizer, showcasing rich flavors and textures. Originating from France, it's popular worldwide, with variations in seasoning and presentation. While it offers a unique dining experience, caution is advised due to the raw meat, requiring careful handling and sourcing for safety.
