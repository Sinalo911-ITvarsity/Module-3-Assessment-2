Double-Stack Mushroom and Chicken Cheeseburger

Prep time: 30 mins

Yield: 1

Price: R125,00

Description

The Double-Stack Mushroom and Chicken Cheeseburger is a tasty burger variation made with a blend of ground chicken and mushrooms. The patties are grilled to perfection, stacked with Muenster cheese, and topped with shredded lettuce, sweet pickles, and curry mustard. Doubling up on patties creates a satisfying meal, perfect for burger night celebrations. Additionally, saving the flavorful liquid from the mushrooms adds depth to other dishes.
