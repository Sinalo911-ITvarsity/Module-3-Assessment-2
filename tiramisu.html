Classic Tiramisu

Prep time: 30 mins

Yield: 6-8

Price: R45,00

Description

Classic Tiramisu is an elegant and indulgent Italian dessert renowned for its layers of coffee-soaked ladyfingers and creamy mascarpone mixture. The ladyfingers are delicately dipped in espresso coffee, which infuses them with rich flavor and moisture. They are then layered with a luscious mixture of mascarpone cheese, egg yolks, and sugar, creating a velvety smooth texture. The dessert is traditionally dusted with cocoa powder, adding a hint of bitterness that balances the sweetness of the mascarpone. Tiramisu is a beloved dessert for its heavenly taste and luxurious texture, making it a perfect finale to any meal.
