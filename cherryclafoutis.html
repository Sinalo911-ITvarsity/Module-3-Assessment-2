Cherry Clafoutis

Prep time: 1 hr 10 mins

Yield: 8

Price: R110,00

Description

Cherry clafoutis is a classic French dessert that features ripe cherries baked in a custard-like batter. The dessert is known for its simple yet elegant presentation, with the cherries peeking out from beneath a golden brown surface. The batter is made with ingredients like eggs, flour, sugar, and milk, resulting in a soft and creamy texture that contrasts beautifully with the juicy cherries. Traditionally, the cherries are left unpitted to infuse the dessert with their almond-like flavor, although some recipes call for pitting them for convenience. Cherry clafoutis is often served warm, either dusted with powdered sugar or accompanied by a dollop of whipped cream or vanilla ice cream. It's a delightful treat that captures the essence of summer with every bite.
